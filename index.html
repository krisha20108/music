Dhvani Bhanushali

Dhvani Bhanushali is an Indian singer. Born in Mumbai,
she gained popularity with her single Vaaste in 2019 which has crossed 1.3 billion views on YouTube,
, making her the youngest Indian pop star to have the fastest 1 billion views on YouTube. She started her music career in 2017
by singing acoustic version song Humsafar from Badrinath Ki Dulhania. Her first song in the film was Ishtehaar from the film Welcome To New York,
released in 2018. In the same year she starred in the music video "Ishare Tere" with Guru Randhawa and "Dilbar" From Satyameva Jayate.
